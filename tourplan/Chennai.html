Day 1: Heritage & Coastal Charm

Daily Itinerary

Morning (9:00 AM - 1:00 PM):

Begin your exploration with a visit to Fort St. George, the first English fortress in India. Explore St. Mary's Church within the fort and delve into colonial history at the Fort Museum. Afterwards, head to the Government Museum Egmore, known for its extensive collection of South Indian bronzes and ancient Roman antiquities.

Lunch:

Savor traditional South Indian Thali or choose from diverse local cuisines.

Afternoon (3:00 PM - 5:30 PM):

Visit Vivekananda House, a heritage building dedicated to Swami Vivekananda, offering cultural insights. Then, proceed to the stunning San Thome Cathedral Basilica, built over the tomb of St. Thomas, one of Jesus' twelve apostles.

Evening (5:30 PM onwards):

Conclude your day with a leisurely stroll along Marina Beach, one of the world's longest urban beaches. Enjoy the sunset and soak in the lively atmosphere.

Specific Attractions

Fort St. George (including St. Mary's Church and Fort Museum)

Government Museum Egmore

Vivekananda House

San Thome Cathedral Basilica

Marina Beach

Food Recommendations

  • Breakfast: Traditional Idli and Sambar, Dosa, or Pongal.
  • Lunch: Try a South Indian Vegetarian "Thali" (Saapadu). Recommended restaurants near Fort St. George/Government Museum: Sree Agarwal Bhojanaalay (Rajasthani Thali), Murugan Idli Shop (Idli/Dosa), A2B Veg Restaurant, Hotel Kalyana Bhavan Biryani (for non-vegetarian biryani).
  • Snacks on Marina Beach: Sample local street food like Sundal.
  • Dinner: Explore Chettinad cuisine (spicy non-vegetarian) or Chennai's distinctive Biryani. Recommended options: Anjappar Chettinad Restaurant or Dindigul Thalappakatti Restaurant.

Day 2: Cultural Immersion & Relaxed Beach Vibes

Daily Itinerary

Morning (9:00 AM - 1:00 PM):

Immerse yourself in the vibrant neighborhood of Mylapore, known for its rich culture and ancient temples. Visit the magnificent Kapaleeshwarar Temple, a 7th-century Dravidian-style temple dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Lunch:

Enjoy authentic South Indian delicacies within the Mylapore area.

Afternoon (3:00 PM - 5:30 PM):

Explore Valluvar Kottam, a chariot-shaped monument dedicated to the classical Tamil poet Thiruvalluvar. Afterwards, unwind at Semmozhi Poonga, Chennai's first botanical garden, offering a serene escape with diverse plant species.

Evening (5:30 PM onwards):

Head to Elliot's Beach (Besant Nagar Beach), a more serene and less crowded alternative to Marina Beach. Enjoy the peaceful ambiance and the promenade lined with cafes. The Ashtalakshmi Temple is also nearby.

Specific Attractions

Mylapore neighborhood

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

Valluvar Kottam

Semmozhi Poonga

Elliot's Beach (Besant Nagar Beach)

Ashtalakshmi Temple (near Elliot's Beach)

Food Recommendations

  • Breakfast: Filter Coffee is a must-try.
  • Lunch in Mylapore: Mylai Karpagambal Mess (famous for keerai vadai, podi dosa, filter coffee), Rayar's Mess (traditional breakfast/tiffin), or Jannal Kadai (known for hot bhajjis).
  • Dinner near Elliot's Beach: The Cascade (Chinese), The Kandyan (Sri Lankan), Eden (vegetarian), or Dindigul Thalappakatti (Biryani). For a unique experience, consider sea-facing restaurants like Bay 6 or Drizzle By The Beach.

Day 3: Wildlife & East Coast Road Exploration

Daily Itinerary

Morning (9:00 AM - 1:00 PM):

Choose your wildlife adventure:

  • Option A: Visit Arignar Anna Zoological Park (Vandalur Zoo), one of the largest zoos in Southeast Asia. (Allocate 3-4 hours due to its size).
  • Option B: Explore Guindy National Park (India's smallest national park within a city) and the adjacent Madras Crocodile Bank Trust (a center for herpetological research). (Allocate 3-4 hours combined).

Lunch:

Dine near your chosen wildlife attraction or proceed towards East Coast Road for a meal.

Afternoon (2:30 PM - 5:30 PM):

Enjoy a scenic drive along the East Coast Road (ECR), known for its picturesque views. Visit the Mahabalipuram Shore Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site featuring ancient rock carvings and temples.

Evening/Dinner (6:00 PM onwards):

Experience a memorable dinner at one of the highly-rated restaurants along ECR, many offering unique ambiances and sea views.

Specific Attractions

Arignar Anna Zoological Park (Vandalur Zoo)

Guindy National Park & Madras Crocodile Bank Trust

East Coast Road (ECR) Scenic Drive

Mahabalipuram Shore Temple

Food Recommendations

  • Lunch near Vandalur/Guindy: Copper Kitchen (family-friendly, biryani), Buhari Hotel (non-vegetarian, biryani), or Sangeetha Veg Restaurant.
  • Dinner along ECR: The Wharf 2.0 (beachfront, seafood), Kai From The Sea (fresh fish, global cuisine), Thoondil (live seafood), The Farm (rustic, organic produce), or ECR Dhaba Restaurant (North Indian).
