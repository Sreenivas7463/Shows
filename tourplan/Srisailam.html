Day 1: Spiritual Immersion & Riverside Charm

Morning

Arrive in Srisailam and check into your accommodation.

Begin your spiritual journey with a customary visit to the Sakshi Ganapathi Temple, where it's believed Lord Ganesha records your pilgrimage.

Proceed to the spiritual heart of Srisailam, the Mallikarjuna Swamy Temple, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva. Take time to admire its intricate Dravidian architecture and soak in the spiritual atmosphere.

Afternoon

Visit the Bhramaramba Devi Temple, one of the 18 Shakti Peethas, located adjacent to the Mallikarjuna Swamy Temple and dedicated to Goddess Parvati.

Explore the impressive Srisailam Dam, a massive hydroelectric project across the Krishna River, offering breathtaking panoramic views of the river and surrounding Nallamala Hills.

Evening

Experience the sacred Pathala Ganga, believed to be an underground stream of the Krishna River with healing properties. You can reach it by descending steps or taking a scenic ropeway ride, which also provides stunning views. Consider an optional boat ride on the Krishna River from Pathala Ganga.

Food Recommendations

Lunch: Enjoy Anna Prasadam (free meals) at the Srisailam temple complex for a simple, satisfying vegetarian meal, or dine at Sri Krishna Bhavan near the Srisailam Temple for South Indian dishes like dosas, idlis, and vadas.

Dinner: Indulge in local delicacies at Hotel Grand Akkamahadevi (try Bobbattu and Miryala Rasam) or Pathala Ganga Restaurant (savor Gongura Pachadi and Vegetable Biryani).

Day 2: Wildlife Adventure & Ancient Wonders

Morning

Embark on an exciting safari at the Nagarjunsagar-Srisailam Tiger Reserve, India's largest tiger reserve. Explore the lush Nallamala Forest, home to diverse wildlife including tigers, leopards, sloth bears, and various bird species.

Afternoon

Take a picturesque motorboat ride from Pathala Ganga to explore the ancient Akka Mahadevi Caves, natural rock-cut caves believed to have been a meditation spot for the revered poet-saint Akka Mahadevi.

Visit the Chenchu Lakshmi Tribal Museum to gain insights into the lifestyle, culture, and livelihoods of the local Chenchu ethnic tribes of Andhra Pradesh. You can also purchase organic honey and souvenirs here.

Evening

Conclude your day with a visit to the Octopus Viewpoint, offering expansive panoramic vistas of the Krishna River as it winds through the dense Nallamala Forest, resembling an octopus with its many arms.

Food Recommendations

Lunch: Dine at Haritha Hotel (a government-run establishment offering a wide range of Andhra cuisine including Pulihora and Pesarattu) or Vijaya Restaurant (serving Andhra, North Indian, and Chinese cuisines) near the Srisailam Bus Stand.

Dinner: Explore Trishul Family Restaurant for Asian and Indian vegetarian options. Enjoy local snacks like hot pakodas or corn on the cob from food stalls near the Srisailam Dam.

Day 3: Majestic Peaks, Waterfalls & Departure

Morning

Start your day by visiting the Shikhareswara Swamy Temple (Sikharam), located at the highest point of Srisailam. Dedicated to Lord Veerashankara, it offers breathtaking panoramic views of Srisailam's landscape and is believed to absolve sins.

Consider a trip to Mallela Theertham Waterfall, a picturesque waterfall cascading down a 150-foot high cliff, located in the dense Nallamala Forest (approx. 58 km from Srisailam).

Afternoon

Visit Paladhara Panchadhara, a beautiful natural spot with perennial streams, considered sacred and associated with Adi Shankaracharya's meditation. It's an ideal place for nature lovers.

Evening

Depending on your departure schedule, spend some leisure time exploring the local markets for traditional handicrafts and souvenirs, or revisit any favorite spots. Enjoy a relaxed lunch or an early dinner before heading back to your onward destination, concluding your enriching Srisailam journey.

Food Recommendations

Lunch: Have a traditional Vegetarian Thali at Rajdhani Restaurant near the Srisailam Bus Stand.

Dinner (pre-departure): Treat yourself to traditional Andhra sweets and snacks at Srisailam Mithaiwala.
