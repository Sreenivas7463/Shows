Abhimanyu

The city of Kaushika is in danger!!! Suddenly the sun is not rising. It is found that Lohar, the iron monster is behind this as he is using his iron island to block the sun rays from entering Kaushika so that he can invade it easily. Without the sun's heat and light, disaster will be created! How will Abhimanyu save his Kaushika?
